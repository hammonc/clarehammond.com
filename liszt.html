Clare and the Piatti String Quartet have just recorded a CD of Mozart, Debussy, Szymanowski and Smetana for Future Classics which is now available on iTunes. Tracks include:

  • Mozart - String Quartet in C major, K. 465 "The Dissonance"
  • Debussy - Images, Book 1 for solo piano
  • Szymanowski - Etudes, Op. 33 Nos. 1-8 for solo piano
  • Smetana - String Quartet in E minor, "From My Life"

Listen to two bonus tracks for solo piano here:

Franz Liszt - 'Les jeux d'eaux à la Villa d'Este'

Franz Liszt - 'Au bord d'une source'

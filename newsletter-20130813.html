Clare will be pianist-in-residence at the Presteigne Festival from the 22-27 August, with a selection of solo, chamber and concerto works. Her programme with violinist Sara Trickey includes pieces by Schubert, Brahms, David Matthews and Joe Duddell while her solo recital features a debut performance of Pearl by composer Robert Peate and a series of miniatures written to commemorate the 150th anniversary of Debussy's birth by James Francis Brown, David Matthews, Peter Fribbins, Alan Mills and Robin Walker. She will perform Shostakovich's Piano Quintet with the Badke Quartet before joining the festival orchestra for Gabriel Jackson's piano concerto in the finale concert.

You can read a review of Clare's recent debut recital at the Cheltenham Festival in the Guardian and view her performance of Schubert, Bach and Ginastera at the Wigmore Hall in June here.

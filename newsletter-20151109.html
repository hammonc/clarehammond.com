Clare makes her film debut as the younger version of Dame Maggie Smith's character, Miss Shepherd, in the Alan Bennett film adaptation The Lady in the Van, released nationwide in cinemas on Friday. Clare performs in a number of flashback scenes recreating a Proms concert in the 1930s, and enacts Miss Shepherd's experiences as a novice nun some years later. Her performances of Chopin and Schubert appear on the soundtrack, released by Sony Classics. You can listen online to an excerpt from Clare's performance of the Schubert on BBC Radio 3's In Tune last week.

Clare's last disc release, 'Etude', continues to garner critical acclaim with the American Record Review describing it as "one of the most enjoyable recordings to come my way this year". Her recent recital for the Swansea International Festival was hailed as a "glittering trove of musical gems" in Seen and Heard International. You can catch her this month in performances in London, Sidmouth, Guildford and Liverpool.

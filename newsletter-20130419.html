Clare will make her debut at Brighton Festival on Wednesday 22 May with a world premiere of Orchids 5 by composer Ed Hughes. Her programme will also include words by Bach, Ginastera and Ravel, with miniatures by Kenneth Hesketh.

Next week she makes her final visit to Belfast as part of her residency at Queen's University and will appear on BBC Radio Ulster's "Sounds Classical" on Sunday evening to discuss her forthcoming recital. With contemporary works by Northern Irish composer Ryan Molloy, and Kenneth Hesketh, alongside sonatas by Schubert and Dutilleux and left-hand works by Bach-Brahms and Lipatti it promises to be an exciting evening!

Clare has just won the 'Young Artist' category at the RPS Music Awards, which celebrate outstanding achievement in 2015. The annual RPS Music Awards are the highest recognition for live classical music in the UK and previous winners of the 'Young Artist' Award include Alina Ibragimova, Gustavo Dudamel, Kate Royal and Edward Gardner. The Awards Ceremony will be broadcast on BBC Radio 3 tonight. Read more here.

Clare will release a new disc with BIS Records of solo piano music by Ken Hesketh on the 27 May. The disc has already been featured on Swiss radio.

Clare's concert schedule over the summer will be quieter than usual as she takes a break to have her baby. You can still catch her in Hay-on-Wye and at the Cheltenham and Presteigne Festivals in July and August.

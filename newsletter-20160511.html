Clare won the 'Young Artist' category at last night's Royal Philharmonic Society Music Awards, which celebrate outstanding achievement in 2015. The annual RPS Music Awards are the highest recognition for live classical music in the UK and previous winners of the 'Young Artist' Award include Alina Ibragimova, Gustavo Dudamel, Kate Royal and Edward Gardner. The Awards Ceremony will be broadcast at 7.30pm on BBC Radio 3 tonight. Read more here.

Her next CD release with BIS Records will come out on the 27 May. The disc, Horae (pro clara), is of solo piano music by Ken Hesketh and has already been featured on Swiss radio.

Clare's concert schedule over the summer will be quieter than usual as she takes a break to have her baby. You can still catch her in Hay-on-Wye and at the Cheltenham and the Presteigne Festivals in July and August.

ODYSSEUS PIANO TRIO

Clare has recently founded the Odysseus Piano Trio with violinist Sara Trickey and cellist Gregor Riddell. Their debut season in 2015 sees them perform at venues across the UK, including St David's Hall in Cardiff, and the Deal, Frome, Llandeilo and Rye Festivals. While their repertoire encompasses core works for trio, they also aim to seek out less familiar pieces and bring them to new audiences.

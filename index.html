RECENT NEWS

The world premiere of pieces by Hamilton Harty, recently given by Clare in Belfast, was featured on BBC Radio Ulster. Terry Blain, in Culture Northern Ireland, wrote 'Hammond recalls both Myra Hess and Annie Fischer, great Beethoven pianists of bygone generations'.
Her recital in April at the Wigmore Hall was broadcast by BBC Radio 3 as part of their 'Live in Concert' programme and her performance of Ravel's Le Tombeau de Couperin was chosen as a Classical Highlight.
Clare's two CDs are now available from Amazon and the iTunes store. Click here for Piano Polyptych, a disc of British contemporary piano music, and here for her CD of Debussy and Szymanowski.

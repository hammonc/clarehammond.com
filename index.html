RECENT NEWS

Clare's disc of piano music by Andrzej and Roxanna Panufnik has just been released with BIS Records. The disc is currently available on eClassical and Amazon.

Clare performed music by Ture Rangström in June as part of a BBC Symphony Orchestra studio concert at Maida Vale, and recorded music by Sibelius for broadcast by BBC Radio 3 later in the year.
Clare will celebrate composer Andrzej Panufnik's centenary in 2014 with a tour of Poland, supported by the British Council, an appearance on In Tune, recital at the Chopin and his Europe Festival, and a festival at Kings Place with the Brodsky Quartet.
Clare made her debut at the Cheltenham Festival in July with a new work by Kenneth Hesketh. Read the Guardian's review here. She also gave world premieres of works by Ed Hughes and Robert Saxton at the Brighton and City of London Festivals last summer.

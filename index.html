RECENT NEWS

Clare made her debut at the Cheltenham Festival early in July with a new work by Kenneth Hesketh. Read the Guardian's review here. She has already given world premieres of works by Ed Hughes and Robert Saxton at the Brighton and City of London Festivals this summer.
Clare's recital in June at the Wigmore Hall for the Park Lane Group was reviewed in BachTrack. Watch her performance of works by Schubert, Bach and Ginastera here.
Catch excerpts from Clare's recent appearances on BBC Radio Ulster's Sounds Classical and BBC Radio 3's In Tune, or listen to two études by Unsuk Chin from her previous broadcast, for Hear and Now in January.
Clare has just embarked on two recording projects with BIS. She will record the complete works of Sir Andrzej and Roxanna Panufnik, and a disc of études by Unsuk Chin, Karol Szymanowski, Sergei Lyapunov and Nikolai Kapustin in September.

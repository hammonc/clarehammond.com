RECENT NEWS

Clare's recording of Sibelius and Rangström at a BBC Symphony Orchestra studio concert was broadcast on Afternoon on 3 in November. She next appears on In Tune on the 20 January to promote her forthcoming disc, Etude, with BIS Records.

Photos from Clare's recent tour of Poland, supported by the British Council, are now available online. The festival Panufnik 100, co-curated by Clare and the Brodsky Quartet, was hailed as the "culmination of the centenary" in the Telegraph.
Clare's disc of music by Andrzej and Roxanna Panufnik, with BIS, has been featured on BBC Radio 3's CD Review and In Tune, selected as Critic's Choice in Gramophone and International Piano magazines, and awarded 5 Diapasons. Read international press reviews here.

Clare recently joined the Philip Langridge Mentoring Scheme, established by the Royal Philharmonic Society, through which she will be mentored by French pianist Anne Queffélec.

RECENT NEWS

Clare's recital in April at the Wigmore Hall with the Piatti String Quartet was broadcast by BBC Radio 3 as part of their 'Live in Concert' programme and her performance of Ravel's Le Tombeau de Couperin was chosen as a Classical Highlight. You can listen to the 'Toccata' here.

An article on Clare's CD Piano Polyptych appeared recently in Classical Music magazine. The disk is now available from Amazon, HMV, and the iTunes store, including works by Julian Anderson, Philip Grange, Piers Hellawell, Kenneth Hesketh and Giles Swayne.

RECENT NEWS

Clare appears on BBC Radio 3's In Tune with violinist Henning Kraggerud on the 7 September. Swiss radio's feature on Etude from earlier in the week is available to stream online.
Clare recorded her third disc for BIS Records in August of music by Ken Hesketh. In September she records two new double concertos for trumpet and piano for Signum Records. Read more here.

Clare performed etudes by Philip Glass to a sold-out Barbican Hall in London in April, alongside the composer himself. Read reviews in the Guardian and the Telegraph.

Clare was 'Rising Star' in March's BBC Music Magazine. Her latest disc, Etude, was featured on BBC Radio 3's In Tune and is Critic's Choice in the Classical Music Magazine. Read reviews here.

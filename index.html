RECENT NEWS

Clare's debut CD, Piano Polyptych for Prima Facie Records includes music by Julian Anderson, Philip Grange, Piers Hellawell, Kenneth Hesketh and Giles Swayne and is due for release in September 2011.

Footage of Clare's recent recital at the Wigmore Hall is now available online.

Clare recently recorded six Etudes by Unsuk Chin for broadcast by BBC Radio 3 in 2012.

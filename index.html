RECENT NEWS

Clare will appear on BBC Radio 3's "In Tune" at 4.30pm on Thursday 27th October to promote her debut CD, Piano Polyptych, playing music by Hesketh, Liszt and Szymanowski.

Piano Polyptych, has just been released by Prima Facie Records, including works by Julian Anderson, Philip Grange, Piers Hellawell, Kenneth Hesketh and Giles Swayne.

Clare recently recorded six Etudes by Unsuk Chin for broadcast by BBC Radio 3 in 2012.

RECENT NEWS

Clare has just completed a tour in Poland premiering a newly discovered concerto by Josef Myslivecek, a mentor of Mozart, with performances in Kalisz, Jelenia Gora and Lublin.

Watch Clare playing works by Chopin, Mendelssohn, Sibelius, Scriabin, Kapustin and Unsuk Chin here.
Clare's recent disc release, Etude, has been selected as 'Critic's Choice' in the American Record Guide. Read reviews here. Her disc of music by Ken Hesketh will be released in May with BIS.

Catch Clare's film debut in Nick Hytner's new Alan Bennett adaptation, The Lady in the Van. You can also hear her on the soundtrack, available on Sony Records.

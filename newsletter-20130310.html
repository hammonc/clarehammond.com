Clare will appear live on BBC Radios 3's In Tune on Monday 18th March at 5.30pm to promote the forthcoming Leftitude Festival with pianist Robert Mitchell. Both will perform pieces for left-hand piano, from the classical and jazz repertoire, prior to their recitals at The Forge in Camden on Thursday 21 March. Clare will be interviewed by pianist Nicholas McCarthy at 7pm, just before her recital.

Other performances in March include a recital at Cardiff University, featuring new pieces by Kenneth Hesketh, and Mendelssohn's first piano concerto with conductor George Vass.

  • 16 March - Mendelssohn First Piano Concerto, Bushey Symphony Orchestra

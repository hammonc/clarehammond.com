Thursday 11 October, Belfast
First visit as Artist-in-Residence at Queen's University Belfast

Clare takes up her position as Artist-in-Residence at Queen's University Belfast for 2012/13 with a three-day visit. Events will include a series of workshops and coaching for undergraduate and postgraduate students on the 9th and 10th October and an evening recital on the 11th for which the programme will be:


Handel - Suite No. 6 in F sharp minor, HWV 431

Hellawell - Piani, Latebre

Beethoven - Sonata No. 23 in F minor, Op. 57 "Appassionata"

Szymanowski - Métopes, Op. 29

Harty - 'Portrait' and 'Spring Fantasy' (WORLD PREMIERES)

Messiaen - Three Preludes

Scriabin - Sonata No. 5, Op. 53

Tickets £12/ £10 concessions available online, or from the Queen's University Welcome Centre.

7.30pm, The Harty Room, Music Building, Queen's University Belfast main site BT7 1NN

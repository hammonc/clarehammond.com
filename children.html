CHILDREN'S CONCERTS and WORKSHOPS

Education and outreach form an important part of Clare's work. In recent years she has given workshops and concerts for children of 4-18 years of age at the Hebden Bridge Piano Festival, Bledington Festival, Newburyport Chamber Music Festival (USA), QEGS Wakefield and the Junior Academy of Music in Belfast.

Clare has just embarked on two recording projects with BIS Records. She is planning to record the complete works of Andrzej and Roxanna Panufnik, and a series of études by Szymanowski, Chin, Lyapunov and Kapustin, in September for release in 2014 and 2015 respectively. Clare will be holding two fund-raising concerts in February, in Cambridge and London. Read more about the étude recording project here.

An excerpt from Clare's recent BBC broadcast of études by Unsuk Chin is now available to listen to on her website. Her next broadcast will be on Radio 3 as part of In Tune in March, promoting the Leftitude Festival, a celebration of left-hand piano music at the Forge in London.

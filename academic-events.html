Academic Events in 2014

26 JANLecture at the Department of Music, University of York
Clare will give a lecture on her doctoral research entitled "The Performer's Prerogative: Paul Wittgenstein and Benjamin Britten's Diversions, op. 21" at 5pm, followed by an evening concert with the Goldfield Ensemble.
7 MARMasterclass at the University of Oxford
Clare will give a masterclass on ensemble playing for music students at the University of Oxford, followed by an evening concert with trumpeter Simon Desbruslais.
11 MARPerformance Class at Bangor University
Clare will give a performance workshop for undergraduate piano students at Bangor University.
16 MARUrban Birds at the INTER/actions Festival, Bangor University
Clare will perform alongside pianists Xenia Pestova and Kathy Supove in the world premiere of Arlene Sierra's Urban Birds for three pianos, electronics and sample birdsong as part of the INTER/actions Festival at Bangor University.
18 MARUrban Birds at Cardiff University
The second performance of Arlene Sierra's Urban Birds at Cardiff University.
27 MARRecital and Lecture at the University of Sussex
Clare will give a recital of works by Bach-Brahms, Mendelssohn, Scriabin and Ginastera at 1.20pm followed by a lecture on her doctoral research entitled "The Performer's Prerogative: Paul Wittgenstein and Benjamin Britten's Diversions, op. 21" at 2.30pm.

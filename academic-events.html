Academic Events in 2013

22 JANLecture as part of the Research Colloquia at the Faculty of Music, Oxford University
"The Performer's Prerogative: Paul Wittgenstein and the enactment of disability in Britten's Diversions, op. 21".
31 JANLunchtime recital and a lecture on left-hand piano music at Manchester University
Clare will perform a lunchtime recital of works for left-hand piano by Robert Saxton, Johannes Brahms and Camille Saint-Saëns followed by Henri Dutilleux's Sonata. She will then give a lecture on left-hand piano music at 2.30pm.
19-21 FEBSecond visit as Artist-in-Residence at Queen's University Belfast
Clare will lead a series of workshops for performance and composition students before giving a lunchtime recital on Thursday 21 February of music by Bach, Liszt, Scriabin, Ginastera and Northern-Irish composer Simon Mawhinney.
9 MARLecture recital at Clare Hall, Cambridge University
Clare will give an evening lecture recital of works for left hand by Brahms and Saint-Saëns alongside two-hand works by Hesketh and Schubert.
12-13 MARRecital and composition workshop at Cardiff University
Clare will give an evening recital of works by Bach, Hesketh, Schubert, Brahms, Saint-Saëns and Dutilleux on the 12th and a composition workshop for undergraduates on the 13th March.
23-25 APRThird visit as Artist-in-Residence at Queen's University Belfast
Clare will lead a series of workshops for performance and composition students, and will give an evening recital on Thursday 25 April of music by Brahms, Hesketh, Schubert, Lipatti, Dutilleux and Northern-Irish composer Ryan Molloy.
3 MAYPerformance workshop at Nottingham University
Clare will lead a performance workshop, with a focus on contemporary music, for undergraduate students.
20 NOVPre-concert talk at Bath Spa University
Clare will give a pre-concert talk on left-hand repertoire at 6.30pm, prior to her evening recital at the Michael Tippett Centre.

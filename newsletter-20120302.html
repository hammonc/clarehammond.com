Clare's five-date tour with the Piatti String Quartet has received a glowing review from the Herald Scotland and is yet to visit St George's Bristol, Farnham Maltings and the Wigmore Hall, London. The CD for the tour is now available on iTunes and Amazon and you can watch an interview with Clare online.

Tracks on the CD include the 'Dissonance' string quartet by Mozart, Images by Debussy for piano, Etudes Op. 33 by Szymanowski and Smetana's quartet, 'From My Life'.

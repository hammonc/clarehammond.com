Clare has been shortlisted for the 'Young Artist' category of the RPS Music Awards, which celebrate outstanding achievement in 2015. The RPS Music Awards, presented in association with BBC Radio 3, are the highest recognition for live classical music in the UK. The 'Young Artist' Award is sponsored by the Bowerman Charitable Trust and winners will be announced at the Awards Ceremony on the 10 May.

Clare's disc Etude was featured as 'Critic's Choice' for 2015 by James Harrington in the American Record Guide. You can watch videos of Clare performing repertoire from the disc here.

Highlights in April include performances of new works for piano and film by Ken Hesketh and Ed Hughes at Kings Place in London and Cardiff University, as well as concerts at St George's Bristol and the Bridgewater Hall in Manchester.

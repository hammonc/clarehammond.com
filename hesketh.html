DISC OF MUSIC BY KEN HESKETH

Clare recorded her third disc for BIS Records, of solo piano music by British composer Ken Hesketh, in August 2015. The disc will be released on May 27 2016 and will include the following works:

▸ Horae (2013) - written for Clare and premiered at the Cheltenham Festival.

▸ Notte Oscura (2002)

▸ Through Magic Casements (2008)

▸ Three Japanese Miniatures (2002)

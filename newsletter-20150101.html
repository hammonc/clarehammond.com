2014 has been a busy year for Clare with the release of her first disc for BIS Records, Reflections, of the piano works of Andrzej and Roxanna Panufnik, a tour of Poland supported by the British Council's Artists' International Development Fund, and the chamber festival Panufnik 100 with the Brodsky Quartet at Kings Place. 'Reflections' was recently nominated 'Critic's Choice' in Gramophone UK while the American Record Guide stated that Clare "performs with authority and great imagination". You can read full reviews here, see photos from the Polish tour here, and photos of 'Panufnik 100' here.

The coming year sees a number of exciting projects including the release of Clare's second disc with BIS, Etude, with music by Chin, Kapustin, Szymanowski and Lyapunov in February. The disc will be launched at the Guildhall School of Music & Drama on the 4 February so please do join us if you're free. Later in the year Clare will premiere a new concerto for trumpet and piano by American composer Geoffrey Gordon with trumpeter Simon Desbruslais and the English Symphony Orchestra under Kenneth Woods. She also makes her film debut as the younger Maggie Smith in a new Alan Bennett adaptation, The Lady in the Van, out in cinemas in November!

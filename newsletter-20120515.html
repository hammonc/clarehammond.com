Clare is performing at the Brighton Festival Fringe this Saturday in a concert organised by Prima Facie Records to promote her debut CD, Piano Polyptych. Her programme features contemporary music by British composers Philip Grange, James Francis Brown, David Matthews and Giles Swayne.

June brings a series of chamber music concerts as Clare collaborates with cellist Richard Birchall, flautist Ana de la Vega and violinist Ruth Palmer. Clare will join Richard on Monday 11th with cello sonatas by Britten and Poulenc at St James's Piccadilly. On Tuesday 12th and Wednesday 13th she gives a programme of flute music by Widor, Poulenc and Prokofiev with Ana de la Vega at two venues in London, St Stephen's Walbrook and 22 Mansfield Street. Then it's to Wye Valley Music in Gloucestershire at the weekend for Bartok and Franck with violinist Ruth Palmer!

  • 19 May - Grange, Brown, Matthews and Swayne at the Brighton Festival Fringe
  • 1 June - Debussy, Ravel, Liszt, Szymanowski and Albeniz at the Royston Arts Festival

  • 12 June - St Stephen Walbrook, London with flautist Ana de la Vega
  • 13 June - 22 Mansfield St, London with flautist Ana de la Vega
  • 16 June - Wye Valley Music, Gloucs with violinist Ruth Palmer

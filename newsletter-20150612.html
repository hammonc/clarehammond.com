Tomorrow, Clare will perform with trumpeter Simon Desbruslais as part of a John McCabe Memorial Concert at the Purcell Room in London. Join them at 7.45pm to hear McCabe's magnificent Sonata after William Byrd's 'Haec Dies' and pay tribute to an inspirational composer.

Simon and Clare recently gave the world premiere of a double concerto, Saint Blue, by American composer Geoffrey Gordon with the English String Orchestra. Inspired by two of Wassily Kandinsky's paintings, this vibrant and dynamic work is a valuable addition to the repertoire. See Simon and Clare discussing the piece here.

Clare's performance of etudes by Philip Glass at a sold-out Barbican Hall in April was well received with the Guardian describing her as "a dazzling athlete". Her album of music by Andrzej and Roxanna Panufnik was commended as "one of the most important fruit of Panufnik's birthday celebrations last year" and her performance described as "outstanding - exuberant, expressive and emotional" in the Polish journal Muzyka 21.

Clare Hammond will join the Piatti String Quartet for the "Future Classics" tour, promoted by the Foreign & Colonial Investment Trust and the Park Lane Group, which runs from February to April. The programme includes piano music by Ravel, Albeniz and Satie with string quartets by Haydn and Debussy. During the five-date tour they will visit the following venues:

21 February - Royal Theatre, Harrogate

27 February - Queen's Hall, Edinburgh

7 March - St George's, Bristol

22 March - Farnham Maltings, Guildford

16 April - Wigmore Hall, London

Full details for each concert are available on Clare's concerts page and you can view the flyer for the tour here.

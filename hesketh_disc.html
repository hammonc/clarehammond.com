HORAE (PRO CLARA), with BIS Records

Clare released her third disc for BIS Records, of solo piano music by British composer Kenneth Hesketh, on the 27 May 2016. This includes four works: Horae (pro clara) (2011/12), Notte Oscura (2002), Through Magic Casements (2008) and Three Japanese Miniatures (2002).

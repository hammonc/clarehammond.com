Clare has a series of chamber music engagements coming up in June with cellist Richard Birchall, flautist Ana de la Vega and violinist Ruth Palmer. Clare's recital at the Wigmore Hall this evening will be broadcast by BBC Radio 3 as part of their 'Live In Concert' programme at 7.30pm tonight. Clare will share the stage with the Piatti String Quartet in the final concert of the "Future Classics" tour, promoted by the Park Lane Group and the Foreign & Colonial Investment Trust, with a programme of Ravel, Haydn, Satie, Debussy and Albeniz. The broadcast will be available on BBC iPlayer throughout the following week.

At the end of the week, Clare will travel to Liverpool and Manchester where she will perform a new work by composers James Francis Brown, Peter Fribbins, David Matthews, Alan Mills and Robin Walker at the Two Rivers Festival in the Wirral and at the Bridgewater Hall in Manchester. The work, Aquarelles, a series of five miniatures, was commissioned to celebrate the Debussy Centenary and receives its premiere on Friday 20 April.

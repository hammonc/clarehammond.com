Thursday 25 April, Belfast
Third visit as Artist-in-Residence at Queen's University Belfast

Clare makes her third visit to Queen's University Belfast as Artist-in-Residence for 2012/13. Over three days she will lead a series of workshops and coaching for undergraduate and postgraduate students and give an evening recital on the 25th for which the programme will be:


Bach-Brahms - Chaconne in D minor, transcribed for left hand

Hesketh - Selection from Horae (pro clara)

Schubert - Sonata in A major, D. 664

Lipatti - Sonatine for left hand

Molloy - Saetre Brygge

Dutilleux - Piano Sonata

Tickets £12 / £10 available online, on 028 9097 5337 or from the General Office in the Music Building.

7.30pm, The Harty Room, Music Building, Queen's University Belfast main site BT7 1NN

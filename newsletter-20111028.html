Clare Hammond appeared on BBC Radio 3's 'In Tune' yesterday to promote her debut CD, Piano Polyptych.

The broadcast is now available on BBC iPlayer until Tuesday 2 November and includes a live interview with Sean Rafferty and music by Hesketh, Liszt and Szymanowski.

Clare's CD includes music by Julian Anderson, Philip Grange, Piers Hellawell, Kenneth Hesketh and Giles Swayne and is currently available from Prima Facie Records.

Clare's Autumn season opens with a new chamber collaboration as she performs trios by Mozart and Françaix in Cambridge with members of the Goldfield Ensemble, the clarinettist Kate Romano and violist Mike Leaver. She then takes a programme of music by Handel, Liszt, Szymanowski, Messiaen and Beethoven's "Appassionata" sonata to music societies in Brentwood and Macclesfield.

October will see her perform a duo programme with violinist Thomas Gould at Alleyn's School in Dulwich. Their programme will include works by Brahms, Wieniawski and nocturnes by Chopin, arranged for violin and piano.

You can also now visit Clare's blog for tales of concerts past!

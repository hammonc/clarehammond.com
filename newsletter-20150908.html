Clare has just recorded a disc of solo piano music by British composer Ken Hesketh, due for release by BIS Records in June 2016. You can view photos from the recording sessions at Cardiff University here. Next week, she makes two world premiere recordings of concertos for trumpet and piano by Geoffrey Gordon and Toby Young. Collaborating with trumpeter Simon Desbruslais and the English String Orchestra, this disc will be released on Signum Records.

The past week has seen two radio broadcasts. Yesterday Clare performed on BBC Radio 3's In Tune with violinist Henning Kraggerud, playing excerpts from Henning's new work, Equinox. Last Wednesday her recent disc release, Etude, was featured on Swiss Radio. The disc continues to attract critical acclaim with the Straits Times describing it as "a revelation".

With composer Ken Hesketh and producer Thore Brinkmann in recording sessions.

Clare's first disc with BIS Records, Reflections, of the solo piano works of Andrzej and Roxanna Panufnik will be released in the UK on Monday 2 June. The CD has been produced to celebrate the centenary of Andrzej Panufnik's birth in 2014 and will be launched at the Polish Embassy. Clare will appear on BBC Radio 3's In Tune later in the month to perform excerpts from the disc and would like to thank the RVW Trust, Polish Cultural Institute, Fidelio Charitable Trust, Vernon Ellis Foundation and the Stradivari Trust for their support of the project. Reflections can be downloaded from eClassical and will be available from online retailers and CD shops in the UK soon.

Later in the month, Clare will perform Mälar-legender by Ture Rangström as part of a BBC Symphony Orchestra studio concert at Maida Vale, and record The Trees by Sibelius for broadcast. She has also been accepted onto a series of masterclasses on Ligeti études with Pierre-Laurent Aimard at Aldeburgh Music. You can read a review of her recital at the Vale of Glamorgan Festival in May on Planet Hugill.

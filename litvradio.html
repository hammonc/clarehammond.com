Clare appeared on BBC Radio 3's 'In Tune' in November with composer George Fenton to promote the premiere of new Alan Bennett film adaptation, The Lady in the Van. Listen to her live performance of Schubert's Impromptu in G flat major, D. 899 No. 3 below.

Her studio recording of the Schubert, and movements from Chopin's Piano Concerto No. 1, are available alongside music specially composed for the film by George Fenton on Sony Records.

Clare Hammond will be appearing on BBC Radio 3's 'In Tune' on Thursday 27th October to promote her debut CD, Piano Polyptych.

Tune in just after 4.30pm to hear a live interview with Sean Rafferty and performances of music by Hesketh, Liszt and Szymanowski. The programme will be available on Radio 3's Audio On Demand for seven days after the broadcast.

Clare's CD includes music by Julian Anderson, Philip Grange, Piers Hellawell, Kenneth Hesketh and Giles Swayne and is currently available from Prima Facie Records.

UPCOMING PERFORMANCES

  • 2 November - Queen's University Belfast - Lecture on Britten's Diversions, Op. 21
  • 3 November - Queen's University Belfast - Liszt, Szymanowski, Panufnik, Hesketh
  • 4 November - City University London - Liszt, Szymanowski, Panufnik, Hesketh

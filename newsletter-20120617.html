July brings two solo recitals in London, at The Dysart Arms in Richmond on the 1st July and at St James's Piccadilly the following evening. Clare will perform Beethoven's 'Appassionata' sonata at both, alongside his set of variations on 'God Save the King' at St James's Piccadilly as part of the Jubilee celebrations. She then visits Lancashire in mid-July to perform at the Ribble Valley International Piano Week ?alongside names?.

Clare will also embark upon a new collaboration with the Constella Ensemble, comprising violinist Tatiana Berman, flautist Ana de la Vega, violist Ruth Gibson, cellist Alexander Chaushian and bassist Elena Hall, in two performances of Bach's Fifth Brandenburg Concerto on the 10th and 15th July in London.

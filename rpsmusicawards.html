RPS MUSIC AWARDS, 2016

Clare has been shortlisted for the 'Young Artist' category of the Royal Philharmonic Society's Music Awards, which celebrate outstanding achievement in 2015. The annual RPS Music Awards, presented in association with BBC Radio 3, are the highest recognition for live classical music in the UK. Awards in 13 categories are chosen by eminent independent juries from the music profession and are unique in the breadth of musical achievement they span â€“ from performers, composers and inspirational arts organisations to learning, participation and engagement. The list of winners since 1989 reads as a roll call of the finest living musicians.

The shortlist was released on BBC Radio 3's In Tune on the 31 March. Winners will be announced at the Awards Ceremony on Tuesday 10 May, with a special RPS Music Awards programme broadcast on BBC Radio 3 on Wednesday 11 May at 7.30pm. The 'Young Artist Award' is sponsored by the Bowerman Charitable Trust.
